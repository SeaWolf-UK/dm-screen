# The Cloaked Coin (Thieves' Guild)

A notorious thieves' guild operating in the shadows of Waterdeep.

## Leadership

- **Guildmaster:** Silas "Shadowfinger" Vane — human rogue, level 12
- **Second:** Mara Quickstep — halfling rogue, level 9

## Operations

The Cloaked Coin controls most of the smuggling routes through the Dock Ward. They specialize in:
- Contraband arcana
- Forged nobility papers
- Information brokering

## Safe Houses

1. The Rusty Anchor (Dock Ward)
2. The Gilded Cage (Trade Ward)
3. The Hollow Oak (City of the Dead)

## Current Plot

The guild has recently acquired a map to the legendary Vault of the Nine, and they are recruiting outsiders for a dangerous heist.
