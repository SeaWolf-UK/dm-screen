# Elminster Aumar

> "I am but an old man who knows a few tricks."

Elminster is one of the most powerful and famous wizards in Faerûn. Despite his age, he remains an active force for good in the Realms.

## Stats

- **Race:** Human (Chosen of Mystra)
- **Class:** Wizard 20 / Cleric 2 / Fighter 2 / Rogue 2
- **Alignment:** Chaotic Good

## Personality

Elminster is cryptic, enigmatic, and often speaks in riddles. He enjoys meddling in the affairs of mortals but only intervenes directly when absolutely necessary.

## Current Whereabouts

Last seen near the Dalelands, investigating strange extraplanar incursions.
